Upload Your Streaming History from Spotify

How to Get Your Streaming History from Spotify?

1. Request Your Data from Spotify

To request your extended streaming history files, follow the steps on the Spotify website:

  1. Open the Privacy page on the Spotify website.
  2. Scroll down to the "Download your data" section.
  3. Configure the page to look like the screenshot below (Untick "Account data" and tick "Extended streaming history").

2. Confirm Your Request

After submitting your request, Spotify will email you a confirmation link. Confirm it to start the data collection process.

3. Wait for Your Data

This process may take some time. Spotify will send a .ZIP file to your email once it's ready.

4. Download and Extract the Files

When your data is ready, download the .ZIP file from the email and extract it to get the .JSON file needed for upload here.
